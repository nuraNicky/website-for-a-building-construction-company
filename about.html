ABOUT US

XYZ Construction is an ISO 9001:2015, EMS 14001:2015 and OHSAS 18001:2007 certified Construction and Engineering Company and is also a member of the SriLankan Green Building Council. XYZ takes tremendous pride in venturing into large and complex projects, embracing the latest technologies and being genuinely engaged in creating the best outcomes for clients and for all stakeholders.


Since 1978, the organization has grown with a clear focus of the vision and mission of delivering high customer service standards and world-class products. The dedication towards Safety, Quality, Delivery, Management, and Cost has made XYZ a leader in the industry.


XYZ provides innovative and sustainable solutions reinforced by robust technology and fully integrated systems. The wide range of services include Design and Build Turnkey Projects, Civil, Infrastructure, Finishing Works and MEP services.


With a most effective development approach throughout every project, XYZ has demonstrated its ability in diverse aspects of the construction industry such as in IT Parks, Commercial, Industrial, Educational, Residential projects and is now making an entry in infrastructure projects.

QUALITY

Safety

XYZ puts safety first above all other management objectives, which in turn has contributed to us having one of the best safety records in the industry. The company has been proactively conceiving, developing and implementing unique safety programs and mechanisms to achieve high standards of safety. The safety programs are proactive and action-oriented; everyone from the management to workman at the site is involved in making workplaces safe. ‘Lead, rather than follow' is the company's approach towards Safety and Statutory Compliance.

SAFETY

Infrastructure

Investment in infrastructure and facilities to execute world-class and highly mechanized construction is a continuous process to provide a significant advantage for the customers. While backed by efficient maintenance, well-equipped and manned centralized store to track, maintain and manage infrastructure, XYZ has a fleet of machinery including tower cranes, mobile tower cranes, batching plants, hoists, earth moving equipment, lab, testing, and fabrication equipment to execute large steel and RCC buildings.
